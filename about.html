WHO WE ARE

Our vision was to create a place for families and locals to come to eat. In the beautifull town of Middletown Ca, we found it challenging to find a family-friendly place to get delicious fresh food. We wanted to provide friends and neighbors with the original form of social networking- a fun, easygoing restaurant, and a cold beer. We believe in providing exceptional quality products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting and that is what has helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best hidden gems in Middletown.
